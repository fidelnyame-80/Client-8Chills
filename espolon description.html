Description

Espolòn Tequila Reposado is a bold and vibrant tequila crafted in the heart of Jalisco, Mexico. Aged for six months in American oak barrels, it delivers a perfect balance of smoothness and character. Known for its striking bottle design featuring iconic Day of the Dead artwork, Espolòn captures both tradition and rebellion. Expect rich notes of caramel, vanilla, and roasted agave, layered with a hint of spice for a warm, lingering finish.

Details

  • Aged: 8 months in oak barrels
  • Alcohol Content: 40% ABV (80 Proof)
  • Volume: 750ml
  • Origin: Jalisco, Mexico
  • Tasting Notes: Vanilla, caramel, citrus, and oak
  • Best Served: Neat or in premium cocktails
